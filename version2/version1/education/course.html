Courses We Offer

provide students with diverse development opportunities

PHD

Sunway University offers PhD programmes that focus on independent research and academic excellence. Students work closely with experienced supervisors to conduct original research and contribute new knowledge to their chosen fields. The programmes prepare graduates for careers in higher education, research, and industry.

Degree

Sunway University offers a wide range of bachelor's degree programmes across various disciplines, including business, computing, engineering, health sciences, hospitality, and the arts. The programmes combine academic knowledge with practical learning to prepare students for professional careers and lifelong learning.

Post Graduation

Sunway University offers a range of postgraduate programmes, including master's and doctoral degrees, across various disciplines. These programmes are designed to deepen students' expertise, strengthen research capabilities, and prepare graduates for leadership roles in academia and industry.

Our Facilities

World Class Library

The Tun Hussein Onn Sunway Library provides students with a wide range of books, journals, and digital resources. It also offers quiet study areas, discussion rooms, and computer facilities to support learning and research.

Largest Play Ground

The outdoor recreational area provides students with a relaxing space for leisure and outdoor activities.

Tasty and Healthy Food

The campus offers a variety of tasty and healthy food options to meet students' different tastes and dietary needs.
